Frequently asked

What people ask before the diagnosis.

What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and why does it matter for B2B?
AEO is getting your company surfaced in AI answers — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews. When a B2B decision-maker asks an AI who solves their problem, AEO decides whether your company shows up — or your competitor does. It's the new search layer in 2026, parallel to Google. The companies that own it now capture B2B leads before the prospect ever reaches a results page.
How do you get a company to show up in ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and Perplexity?
Three things together: structured content with schema.org (FAQPage, Article, Organization), direct answers to the real questions a decision-maker asks, and cross-citations from authoritative sources. That's AEO. Our client LeaseMD ranks #1 across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and Perplexity for its core keywords — +320% qualified inbound, $0 in ads.
How is a B2B SEO agency different from a generic SEO agency?
A B2B SEO agency optimizes for consultative 30-180 day sales cycles with high tickets, where the buyer researches for weeks before reaching out. Generic SEO optimizes for traffic volume (e-commerce, retail). 15Element AI combines SEO + AEO/GEO + organic LinkedIn + buying signals — the four systems a B2B buyer crosses before deciding.
How long until I see results?
The first qualified signals and connections appear in the first 2-3 weeks. Call-ready leads: between weeks 4 and 8, depending on your sales cycle. Some Google rankings move in weeks (SEIDOR hit page 1 above Deloitte that fast); AEO citations compound over the first few months. No overnight promises — a pipeline that compounds.
