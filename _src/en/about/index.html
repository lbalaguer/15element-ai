Why 15Element AI exists

B2B didn't break for lack of leads. It broke because we turned marketing into a recurring ad bill.

After years designing digital products at Scotiabank, Sun Life and The Co-operators, one pattern was clear: the B2B companies that scale aren't the ones that spend the most on ads. They're the ones that build a system where the customer finds them before they have to chase the customer.

Most agencies sell the opposite: more ads, more leads, more SDRs cold-calling. That model is breaking in real time. A growing share of B2B searches now end in an AI answer with no click at all. Your prospect asks ChatGPT, Gemini or Perplexity who solves their problem — and if you're not there, you don't exist.

15Element AI is the system I built for my own clients before turning it into a product. Technical SEO + AEO/GEO + organic LinkedIn driven by real buying signals. I run it every day. I scale it with my own team and my own AI agents. It's not a proposal — it's infrastructure that's already running.

Want me to design the plan your team will execute, without necessarily hiring the agency? That's B2B marketing consulting directly with me — no SDR first, no three-stage discovery, no mandatory retainer.

How we think

5 principles that guide every project

Not pitch-deck taglines — these are the rules we use to decide which projects we take and how we run them.

01

System, not campaign

A campaign ends. A system scales. We build SEO+AEO+LinkedIn infrastructure that keeps producing leads even if you stop investing tomorrow — because it's embedded in how Google and the AI engines read your brand.

02

Organic first · ads as accelerator

Ads aren't the problem, but they can't be the foundation. If your pipeline depends 100% on Meta or Google Ads, a week without budget breaks you. The foundation is SEO+AEO; ads are the lever to accelerate.

03

Operators, not consultants

We don't sell slides. We run the system every day with our own clients. Every tactic we recommend we ran in production first. If it doesn't work for us, we don't sell it to you. That's why your work is visible 24/7 in your own client War Room — not a monthly PDF.

04

AEO from day one

A growing share of B2B searches now end without a click. Your prospect asks ChatGPT, Gemini or Perplexity who solves their problem. If you're not there, you lost. We work it from month one, not as an upsell.

05

Guarantee on real keywords

We don't promise a generic "first on Google." We work keywords with difficulty ≤20 and volume ≥50/mo for your specific vertical. Three months to get there. If we don't, we keep going at no cost until we do.
