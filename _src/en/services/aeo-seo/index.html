Frequently asked

SEO, AEO & GEO for B2B companies.

What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)?
AEO is the practice of getting your company surfaced and cited in AI answer engines — ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity and Google AI Overviews — when a buyer asks who solves their problem. It runs parallel to traditional SEO and increasingly decides which vendors a B2B buyer ever considers. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the closely related discipline of being the source those engines pull from when they generate an answer.
How do you get a B2B company cited by ChatGPT and Perplexity?
Three things working together: structured content with schema.org (FAQPage, Article, Organization), direct answer-first responses to the real questions a decision-maker asks on every page, and a consistent, authoritative company entity with cross-citations the models trust. Our client LeaseMD ranks #1 across ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity for its core queries — +320% qualified inbound, $0 in ads.
How long until I see results in AI engines like ChatGPT?
It varies by competition and starting authority. Some Google rankings move in weeks (SEIDOR hit page 1 above Deloitte that fast). AI citations typically build over the first few months as topical authority and entity signals accumulate. We set an honest timeline in the diagnosis based on your specific niche — no "ranked in ChatGPT in 30 days" promises.
Does ranking in AI engines replace Google SEO?
No — it adds to it. Google is still where a huge share of B2B research happens, and strong SEO authority is one of the signals AI engines use to decide who to cite. The two compound: SEO authority feeds AEO, and AI citations drive brand searches that feed SEO. That's why we build both at once rather than betting on one.
Does this work for niche B2B companies with low search volume?
Yes — niche B2B is where it works best. Low search volume means low competition, and AI engines are especially valuable in specialized categories where buyers ask detailed, conversational questions a keyword tool never captures. BCS Bioclean ranks #1 for "passivation vs pickling steel systems" — tiny volume, exactly the right buyer, and now cited in AI answers. In B2B, the right ten people beat ten thousand wrong ones.
